Privacy & ethics

The Arts Wire listens to the public life of art. We read public arts journalism, public artist statements, open archives, and open listings, and we write original summaries that always link back to the source. We do not enter private spaces, we do not bypass paywalls, we do not copy full articles, and we do not imitate any living writer.

We do not sell your data, and we do not run tracking-based advertising. If you subscribe, we keep only what we need to send you the wire and the letter, and you can leave at any time.

Every artwork we show is from a public-domain or open collection, credited to its museum. If you ever feel we have represented your work unfairly, write to us and we will listen.
